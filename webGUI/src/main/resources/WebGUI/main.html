Welcome to the MisTiGriD Web User Interface

Getting started:

  1. Configure goals :
    • house power goal (upper left corner : "Change goal: ").
    • comfort goal (click on the manager of a heater or lamp : or ).
  2. Observe the autonomic system trying to achieve the new goals:
    • Orange managers ("Saving" mode) change to Green (reducing power or temperature goals), as needed to help achieve the goals.
    • Red managers ("Comfort" mode) do not contribute.
    • Check out the live graphs displaying goals, power consumptions, temperature
  3. Play with the environment's temperature (upper right corner ), click on lamps to switch them on and off, try different combinations of comfort and power goal.
Please refer to the manual for further details on using the simulator.
